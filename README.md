# Behind My Eyes
## Description
Behind My Eyes (Combat) is a prototype for our thesis project made in Godot 4.6. It's a 2D pixel art PVE card game with mechanics similar to pokemon mixed with rock paper scissors.

## Deck
Each card has a star cost of 1 to 3 and a card type connotation.
The connotations are:
- Sad
- Angry
- Happy
- Nice
- Sarcastic

Each player makes a deck of up to 10 cards with a total star cost of 20.

## Combat
- You compete vs an AI to either both reach collaboration or one of you to Win first by accumulating points.
- One player first plays a card then the other, when both cards are played both player's card effects trigger and they gain points based on the type of the other card.
- Card effects can be gaining extra points, creating status effects or a completely unique effect.
- Every time you play a card it goes to the discard pile for 2 turns then is shuffled back into your deck
- You have 45 seconds to play each card.
